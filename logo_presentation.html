Visual Metaphor & Concept

Raw Data → AI → Action

The continuous geometric line flows left-to-right. It starts as a jagged wave (Midnight Petrol Blue) mapping erratic healthcare datasets, steps up to an upward slope (Emerald Jade) validating predictive insights, and resolves at a high point with a solid dot (Copper Amber) signaling trusted, precise action.

Timeless Minimalist Layout

By using clean vector paths, flat colors, and geometric letter proportions, the logo remains completely legible at all scales, from a 16x16 browser favicon to a large-format public sector presentation.
